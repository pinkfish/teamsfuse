The Eastside Travel League promotes great sportsmanship and healthy youth basketball competition, while providing the opportunity for community based programs to develop players and teams to compete against one another.

Girls League Directors Meeting Sept. 13

The first ETL girls program directors meeting will be held at 7:00 pm, Sept. 13, at Newport High School Room 1112. Agenda includes:

  • Season Schedule
  • Dues
  • Game Day Management
  • Officials
  • Parent Behavior Expectations
  • Playing Rules

Boys League Directors Meeting Sept. 27

The ETL boys season opens with a directors meeting at 7:00 pm, Sept. 27, at Newport High School Room 1112. Agenda includes:

  • Season Schedule
  • Dues
  • Game Day Management
  • Playoffs Hosting
  • Officials
  • Parent Behavior Expectations
  • ETL/AAU
  • Open Discussion

GYM AVAILABILITY FORMS DUE OCT. 24

Each program must submit at least 9 home dates to host games. ETL open dates for the boys and girls leagues are:

  • Dec: 1st, 2nd, 8th, 9th, 15th, 16th
  • Jan: 5th, 6th, 12th, 13th, 26th, 27th  (no games over MLK)
  • Feb: 2nd, 3rd (mornings only, Super Bowl Sunday) 9th, 10th 23rd, 24th (No President's Day weekend)
  • March: 2nd, 3rd (final weekend of girls regular season play)
  • ETL Boys Playoffs: March 1st, 2nd, 3rd
  • ETL Girls Playoffs: March 8th, 9th, 10th

Gym availability forms can be downloaded below and should be returned to the ETL at commissioner@eastsidetravelleague.org
